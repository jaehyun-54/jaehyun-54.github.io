ABOUT ME

Exploring robots that can adapt, transform, and hold their shape.

My research focuses on robotic mechanisms that can adapt their shape and configuration to complex environments.

I am currently developing a modular continuum robot with independently lockable segments. The system is designed to create diverse configurations and maintain its posture without continuous power consumption.

I also study vision-based workspace evaluation, SMA actuator characterization, thermal response, repeatability, and energy consumption.
