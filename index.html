INTRODUCTION

Robots that move, adapt,
and hold their shape.

My research explores robotic mechanisms that can adapt their shape and configuration to complex environments.

My current work focuses on a modular continuum robot with independently lockable segments. The robot can create multiple configurations and maintain its posture without continuous power.

I also study vision-based workspace evaluation and the displacement, thermal response, repeatability, and energy consumption of SMA-based actuation systems.
