PROFILE

About Me

I am a graduate student in Mechanical Engineering at Korea University, working on robotic mechanisms that can adapt their shape and configuration.

My current research focuses on a modular continuum robot with independently lockable segments. The system is designed to generate diverse configurations and maintain its posture without continuous power consumption.

I am also interested in medical robotics, shape-morphing mechanisms, smart actuators, vision-based robot evaluation, and energy-efficient robotic systems.
