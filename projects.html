CONTINUUM ROBOTICS

Shape-Lockable Continuum Robot

A modular continuum robot with independently lockable segments and zero-power posture maintenance.

Each module uses an SMA-actuated release mechanism and a mechanical locking structure, allowing selected segments to move while the remaining segments maintain their configuration.

  • Continuum Robot
  • SMA Actuator
  • Mechanical Locking
  • Modular Design
PROJECT 02 Add your workspace figure

COMPUTER VISION

Robot Workspace Evaluation

A vision-based experimental system for tracking robot motion and evaluating the reachable workspace of a modular continuum robot.

Multiple cameras and OpenCV are used to measure end-tip trajectories, rotational motion, and three-dimensional workspace characteristics.

  • OpenCV
  • Camera Calibration
  • Workspace
  • Motion Tracking
PROJECT 03 Add your SMA experiment image

SMART ACTUATORS

SMA Actuator Characterization

Experimental characterization of SMA wire actuation under different wire diameters, voltage conditions, and restoring spring coefficients.

The evaluation includes displacement, response time, recovery time, temperature, repeatability, and energy consumption.

  • SMA Wire
  • Displacement
  • Thermal Response
  • Repeatability
PROJECT 04 Add your energy graph

ENERGY ANALYSIS

Robot Energy Consumption

Measurement and analysis of voltage, current, power, and cumulative energy consumption during robot actuation.

The system compares energy consumption during unlocking, shape transformation, relocking, and posture maintenance.

  • INA226
  • Arduino
  • Power Measurement
  • Energy Analysis
